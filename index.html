intro

Hello, I am "utenor", I have created this website for many reasons.
I used to get bored at home a lot, so I decided to take up learning Web Development. That was around 2021-22.
I launched my first accessible website, huracan.dev.
However, I gave up after a while since I never could quite get my website to work how I wanted it to.
It was a great learning experience though and now I have created "utenor.com".
I am usually not interested in programming and all that jazz but web development is more creative, much easier as well since its "coding" and not "programming".
I decided to come back to web development as I'm now bored again with nothing to do at home.
Hopefully, this time I'll learn even more and create some fun projects and maybe even create services for people like you reading this.
